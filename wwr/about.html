What Makes Us Who We Are?

We Were Made for the Adventure.

We are all about the adventure, but more than that. We are about bringing people together for the adventure. We started Dry Oar Rafting to create memories for the whole family. in 1950 our family set out on the river with our homade rafts and since we've been all about the adventure and the family. We weren't satisfied with just making these memories for our family, but we wanted to share those experiences with as many people as we could. That's why we started this company and we hope you'll be able to join us. Please click the link to book your adventure now!
