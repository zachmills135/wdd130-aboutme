Overview

Purpose.

To bring families and friends closer together by helping them have memorable rafting experiences together. We seek to build revenue by providing appointments for families to book rafting trips together.

Audience

The audience is families and frineds, groups of people between 12 - 45 years of age with a stable, steady, and good income. They will not have to worry about planning a trip for their whole family. We target the heads of the families, i.e. parents because they are the ones that are most capable to organize a trip with their whole family. They want exciting and fond memories of their family having fun togehter. They will normally access the site via computer or smartphone.
